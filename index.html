Introduction

Sample App is a simple, yet category-defining "listen and type" game, designed to improve any user's English vocabulary.

User Guide

Play the game: (Requires active internet connection)

  1. Log in/Register
  2. Select Play to launch the fun!
    • A word will be read out by our beautiful-sounding South African AVSpeechSynthesizer, Tessa.
    • The definition of the word will be on display (courtesy of Wordnik.com)
    • Type what you think you heared in the text field.

Game rules

  • Word must be spelt correctly from start to finish to get full points. (20 points per letter)
  • You may press the "?" to let us fill in one letter for you. (-25 points per clue)
  • Pressing "Skip" will choose a new word of the same difficulty level. (-10 points per letter)
  • Pressing "Speak Again" will make Tessa read the word out again. (No penalty)
